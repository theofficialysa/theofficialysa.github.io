Projects

Each project was thoughtfully formulated and approved by the founders of the
Youth Sustainabiliy Alliance.

1

RENEWABLE ENERGY RESEARCH

YSA is collaborating with an NYU Abu Dhabi professor to conduct research focused on identifying the most suitable renewable energy sources for powering the UAE's vehicles. We aim to provide valuable insights into sustainable transportation options and contribute to a greener future.

2

ENERGY POLICY PROPOSAL

YSA is creating a comprehensive policy proposal based on the findings from our renewable energy research. This proposal outlines recommendations for governments and international organizations to promote and implement renewable energy in the transportation sector.

3

TECH/E-WASTE DRIVE DONATIONS

YSA will organize a tech/e-waste drive to collect electronic devices and equipment. The collected items will be refurbished and donated to countries in need, including Lebanon, Tanzania, and potentially Palestine, to bridge the digital divide.

4

ENERGY DOCUMENTARY

YSA will produce a documentary focused on renewable energy initiatives and their impact. The documentary will be showcased at conferences, including COP29, to raise awareness and inspire action.

5

PLANTING THE UAE'S MANGROVES

YSA will support the UAE government's goal of planting 100 million mangroves by 2030. This may involve organizing mangrove planting events, partnering with local environmental agencies, and engaging the youth community in ecological restoration efforts.

6

YOUTH ENGAGEMENT

YSA will collaborate with NGOs like ClimateFresk to conduct workshops and educational sessions to engage youth in environmental issues. These workshops will cover various topics related to sustainability, climate action, and environmental awareness.

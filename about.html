Who are we?

Our dedicated team of experienced executives is at the heart of what we do, utilizing their unique skills and passion to move the work of our Organization forward. Without them, YSA would not be where it is today. Meet some of our team members below.

Sumin Bae

(Co-Founder/Co-Director/Chief Executive Officer)

Hello, I'm Sumin Bae, a high school student based in Abu Dhabi. I'm honored to be a co-founder and director of the Youth Sustainability Alliance (YSA), a platform I'm deeply passionate about. My passion for environmental sustainability started when I was young. When I was 12 years old, I came across a documentary about pollution and the threat that it poses to society. Since then, I have begun reading more books and small articles about ways to mitigate pollution. My concern grew larger when my family faced severe medical conditions due to respiratory diseases caused by fine dust in South Korea. As I became more involved in initiatives related to environmental sustainability, I realized that the best way to make a difference is to start my own organization with my own ideas.

Marcus Ayoub

(Co-Founder/Co-Director/Chief Executive Officer)

Hello. My name is Marcus Ayoub and I’m a student at the American Community School of Abu Dhabi. As the founder of the YSA, I envision a world rebuilt. A healthy, vibrant, and sustainable world through the sophisticated projects we have planned for the YSA. As a child, a love grew in me for nature. I was exposed to the healthier, more luscious parts of the environment. Though, as I grew and gained perspective on the globe, I realized the world is nowhere near the sustainable health it could potentially reach. I felt as if I had a duty to this world, one which I aim to improve the state of the worlds health in numerous aspects. The best way I believed I could’ve done so, is gathering a group of people committed to improving the state of the world. The YSA.

Roan Maguire-Shashoua

(Founder of Berkeley Branch/Secondary Director)

Hi, I’m Roan Maguire-Shashoua, and I founded YSA Berkeley, located at Berkeley High School in Berkeley, CA. Sustainability and environmental action have always been of utmost importance to me. Stopping climate change has a deadline the world cannot miss, and the steps needed to succeed in doing so are monumental. So without everyone getting involved, the healthy planet we all envision is not guaranteed. That’s why I believe in YSA; it empowers the youth–the demographic with the least amount of political and economic power–to make a meaningful impact on the issue that impacts us the most. Through measured action such as political engagement, and community education, we will make a difference on a global scale, and start a positive chain of youth engagement to drive change.

Jae Kim

(Waste Management Officer)

Hello, my name is Jae Kim and I am a senior in the class of 2024 at ACS. My passion for environmental sustainability stems from one of my other passions: computer science. As an avid coder and tech enthusiast, I am constantly exposed to the waves of new technology that entice consumers every year. While advancements in technology are exciting, they also come at the expense of the environment. 50 million tons of electronic waste are generated annually. In some parts of the world, that figure is expected to increase by 500%. As a part of YSA I hope to address this global issue by raising awareness in our community and globally; researching possible avenues to reduce electronic waste before it happens; and organizing tech drives to collect, recycle; and donate electronics.
